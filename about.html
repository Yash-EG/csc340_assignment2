About This Project

Why Antiheroes?

I chose the antihero theme because these characters represent the complexity of human nature. Unlike traditional heroes, antiheroes operate in moral gray areas, making difficult choices that challenge our understanding of right and wrong. From Johnny Silverhand's rebellion against corporate tyranny to Joel Miller's desperate fight for survival, these characters resonate because they're flawed, relatable, and deeply human.

Design Choices

The glassmorphism design aesthetic was chosen to create a modern, sleek interface that feels futuristic yet accessible. The semi-transparent cards with backdrop blur effects give depth to the layout while maintaining readability. Each character has unique color schemes that reflect their game's visual identity - yellow and cyan for Cyberpunk 2077, green tones for The Last of Us, and so on.

The page was build using 2560*1440 pixels screen, so thats the best resolution to check the website in. It is somewhat responsive, but the cards are not.
