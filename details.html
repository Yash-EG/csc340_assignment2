// BACKSTORY

Johnny Silverhand was a legendary rockerboy and frontman of the band Samurai. In 2023, he led an assault on Arasaka Tower to destroy their secure database. The mission failed, and Johnny was killed by Adam Smasher. His consciousness was later stored on the Relic biochip, eventually merging with V's mind in 2077.

// ABILITIES

Combat Veteran Silverhand Arm Charismatic Leader Guitar Skills

// PERSONALITY

Rebellious, anarchistic, and fiercely anti-corporate. Johnny is charismatic but self-destructive, driven by hatred for Arasaka and a desire for revolution.
